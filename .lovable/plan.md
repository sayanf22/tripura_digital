

## Tripura Digital Technologies — Digital Marketing Website

### Brand & Design
- **Colors**: White dominant background with blue accents (primary blue #2563EB, dark blue #1E3A5F for headings)
- **Style**: Modern, minimal, clean with rounded corners (xl/2xl radius), smooth animations, micro-interactions
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, strong hierarchy

### Pages & Routing

1. **Home** — Hero with animated text + CTA buttons, services overview cards, "Why Choose Us" stats counter, client logos/trust badges, testimonials carousel, CTA banner, footer
2. **About** — Company story, mission/vision, team section, achievements
3. **Services** — Individual cards for each service with hover animations:
   - Website Development
   - SEO & Digital Marketing
   - Social Media Marketing
   - Social Media Management
4. **Portfolio** — Project showcase grid with filter animations
5. **Blog** — Articles grid (placeholder content for now)
6. **Contact** — Contact form, Google Maps embed placeholder, company info, social links

### Animations & Micro-interactions
- Scroll-triggered fade-in/slide-up animations on all sections
- Animated hamburger menu (X transform) with slide-in mobile nav
- Hover effects on cards (scale, shadow lift)
- Animated counters for stats (projects completed, clients, etc.)
- Smooth page transitions
- Button hover ripple/scale effects
- Floating CTA (WhatsApp/phone) button with pulse animation

### Navigation
- Sticky header with blur backdrop, logo left, nav links right
- Animated hamburger icon on mobile → full-screen or slide-in menu with staggered link animations
- Active link indicator

### Call-to-Action Optimization
- Hero section: "Get a Free Quote" + "Our Services" buttons
- Each service card: "Learn More" CTA
- Floating WhatsApp/Call button
- Contact form on every page footer area
- "Get Started" CTA banner before footer

### Footer
- Company info, quick links, services links, contact details, social media icons, copyright
